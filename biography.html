Biography

Short Bio

  • I am Wei Zhang, currently a Zijiang Young Scholar (Associate Researcher) in the Department of Data Science and Engineering, School of Computer Science and Software Engineering, East China Normal University (ECNU). Before that, I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University, and my advisor was Prof. Jianyong Wang in database group. In the period of 2015.01-2015.07, I was a visiting student in UIUC, advised by Prof. Jiawei Han. I received the B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2011. In the period of 2010.11-2011.06, I was a member of HIT-CIR and advised by Prof. Bing Qin.
