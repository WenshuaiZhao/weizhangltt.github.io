Biography

Short Bio

    I am Wei Zhang, currently a Zijiang Young Scholar (Associate Researcher) in the Department of Data Science and Engineering, School of Computer Science and Software Engineering, East China Normal University (ECNU).

    I got my Ph.D. in the department of Computer Science and Technology from Tsinghua University, and my advisor was Prof. Jianyong Wang from database group. I received my B.Sc. degree, with the major of information security in the department of Computer Science and Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology in 2011.

    I visited the data mining research group in Computer Science, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, under the supervision of Prof. Jiawei Han from January 2015 to July 2015. I was also a member of HIT-CIR and advised by Prof. Bing Qin from Octorber 2010 to June 2011.
